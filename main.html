About Us

Welcome to
Billimoria Farm

4 Bedrooms pet friendly Villa with a Swimming pool, lawns, pool table and wifi.

* Weekdays 20k/night
* Weekends 25k/night plus
* 15% booking handling charges
* Accommodation is for 10 to 12 people(Extra person is chargeable)
* Food is not included

Restaurant is right across our Farm and home cooked meals also available on request with additional charges.There are two options:-
1. Village Restaurant which is top Veg / Non Veg Restsurant of Karjat is just opposite to the farm. The Menu and Rate Card is available at the farm. You can order it at the farm can enjoy your food there.
2. There is a person at the farm who stays 100 mtrs away can give you Veg / Non Veg Food at the farm. He makes the food as per your requirement "Home Food" and brings it to serve you. There is Microwave at the farm too.

Payment mode:- Cash 60% in advance with date confirmation, 40% balance on site while entering premises with registration.

Also there is a couple to attend you at the farm for housekeeping and look after guest max till 11.00.pm
Book early to avoid disappointment

Rules

Be expected every guest Follow the rules, otherwise Any Damage Property/ Electronics/ art will be paid for as actual during checkout

* Any Damage done to property / electronics / art will be paid for as actual during checkout.

* Please do not smoke or eat in the bedrooms. The guests shall not do any thing in the said premises which is likely to cause any nuisance.

* No consuming/ carrying drugs, carrying firearms/ weapons or conduct any unlawful activities in such case we reserve the right to request any guest to vacate the property forthwith, Without previous notice and assigning any reason whatsoever, the guest shall be bound to vacate when requested to do so and will be reported to the local authorities

* Kindly clean up after your pet. We believe food, water and electricity is very precious, please do not waste.
